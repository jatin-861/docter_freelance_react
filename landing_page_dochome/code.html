Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical arrival time? expand_more

Our doctors typically arrive within 45-90 minutes for urgent requests, or exactly at your scheduled appointment time for routine visits.

Are your doctors certified? expand_more

Every DocHome doctor is MCI certified with a minimum of 5 years of clinical experience and has passed a rigorous background check.

Which cities are you currently in? expand_more

We currently serve Mumbai, Delhi NCR, Bangalore, and Pune, with plans to expand to more Tier-1 cities soon.

Do you provide care for children? expand_more

Yes, we have specialized pediatric care teams who are experts in managing child health issues in a comfortable home setting.

How do follow-ups work? expand_more

After every visit, you get a 24-hour window for free chat follow-ups with your doctor through our mobile app.
